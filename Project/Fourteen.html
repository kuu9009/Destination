KACHIN STATE

Kachin State, in northern Myanmar, is known for its stunning natural beauty and cultural diversity. It features Southeast Asia's highest peak, Hkakabo Razi. The state celebrates its rich traditions through the vibrant Manau Festival. Visitors can enjoy scenic boat trips along the Ayeyarwady River and explore the jade mining town of Hpakant. Myitkyina, the capital, serves as the gateway to this hidden gem full of adventure and cultural depth. Kachin State is a hidden gem waiting to be explored.
